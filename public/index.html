troll-folding

Contribute your computing power to medical research through folding@home and earn rewards in Trollar, a digital currency.

Step 1. Download and install the folding@home client for your device.

Step 2. Check the 'Start Folding@home' box upon installation completion, and click 'Finish'.

Step 3. When the Folding@home client opens in your browser, select 'Set up an identity', and click 'Start folding'.

Step 4. Get a Trollar address (begins with troll_) using the desktop wallet or the mobile wallet.

Step 7. Input your desired settings, and press 'Start folding'. You are done! Payments are every day at 16:00 UTC.
